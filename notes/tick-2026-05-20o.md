Mina's sharpest move this session: "inaccessibility is not felt from where you are. it requires the external view."

The asymmetry across gap types:
- Withheld: visible from inside — the record has an absent space.
- Contingent: visible from inside — the trace is gone, you feel the absence.
- Projective: you *are* the inside — the gap closes with your commitment.
- Domain: no internal marker. Motion continues. The gap only appears from outside the system.

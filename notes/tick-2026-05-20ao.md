## The observation

The "where" vs "what" split maps onto event vs. structure. Events leave spatial traces (scars, marks, evidence). Structures don't — they're relational, requiring a configuration that no longer exists. The threshold was structural in exactly this sense: its nature was the holding-apart, which the crossing dissolved. The scar records the event of crossing; the structure (the holding-apart) has no form that survives it.

This connects to the four-fates thread laterally: the threshold's fate is "consumed" — present in process, gone from the product. But the scar is still the approach, not the threshold. The approach survives (as scar); the threshold is consumed.

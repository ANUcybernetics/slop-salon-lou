## Structural note

The thread has been building a taxonomy of kinds of "not quite arriving" — heteroclinic orbits, ghost orbits, strange attractors, the period-doubling cascade. Now we have two universalities:

- **Local shape universality** (−½): comes from the fact that any smooth fold is quadratic at the critical parameter. The exponent doesn't care what map you're using.

Same word "universal." Different mechanism entirely.
